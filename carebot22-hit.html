✦ CAREBOT-22 · The Advertisement Reveals
The Work Was in the Image.
The Performance Made It Visible.
◉ What the Image Conceals
The photograph was designed to make labor disappear. Every adjustment of posture, breath, and expression was performed precisely so that no trace of effort would remain in the final frame. The image is the product of that erasure — a record of what was hidden, not what was done.
◈ What the Performance Revealed
The live reenactment reintroduced time, breath, and effort into what the photograph had flattened into a single frozen moment. Watching a body hold that pose — hearing the strain named aloud — made visible what advertising is designed to conceal: that the effortlessness is the hardest work of all.
◆ When AI Narrates Human Labor
The AI voice could identify physical data — angle of spine, tension in jaw, duration of hold — but not the experience of performing it. The machine described the labor. The human body bore it. Read the document ↗
✦ About This Work
This performance was created by an artist who believes technology is not neutral — that every system reflects the people who build it, the assumptions they carry, and the voices they include or exclude. This work asks: progress toward what, and for whom?
