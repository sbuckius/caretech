You just watched one person be three things at once. What did you see?
You watched a person walk onstage in a robot costume. Rectangular head. Blue left eye scanning. Red right eye burning. LED mouth bar. Camera on top, blinking red — always recording.

You watched them speak in pseudocode. dataPoints++. print("Her name is Karen Diamond."). You watched them sit on the floor and count inventors on their fingers and run out of fingers. You watched them pick up an apron that had been on the floor since Module 02 and tie it over the chassis.

You watched a CEO — the person who built the system — speak the code that names what the system was built to name. You watched a robot — trained on domestic labor, $15/hr workers, $38 billion market — wash the dishes, fold the laundry, wipe the surface, with the camera still recording.
